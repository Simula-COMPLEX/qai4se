About the Project

QAI4SE explores the intersection of quantum computing, artificial intelligence, and software engineering. Our goal is to leverage quantum-enhanced AI methods to solve complex problems in software analysis, testing, and optimization.

Who We Are

This project is developed under the Simula Complex Systems research group. We collaborate across disciplines to bring the power of quantum technology into practical SE applications.
